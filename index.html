About Paris ACTS

Paris ACTS is created to reinforce the links between different research groups in the Paris area which are working on the connections between automata theory and concurrency, and in order to advance this research on a national and international level,

The seminar will take place for an afternoon every six weeks, moving between LIX (École Polytechnique), LRE (EPITA Paris), and IRIF (Université Paris Cité). It will be held in hybrid mode and builds on the extensive experience of U.Fahrenberg and J.Ledent in organizing the (i)Po(m)set Project Online Seminar (PPOS), of which it is, in a sense, a continuation.

Paris ACTS is inspired by the MeFoSyLoMa seminar, with which it has a slight thematic overlap, but includes a hybrid component. (We will avoid scheduling conflicts with MeFoSyLoMa.)

The program for each afternoon will be as follows:

14:00--14:45 In-person talk
14:45--15:30 Virtual talk
15:30--16:00 Coffee break
16:00--17:00 Discussion
