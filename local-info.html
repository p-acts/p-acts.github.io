Location

The seminar will rotate between three venues:

  • IRIF: Université Paris Cité, Paris, France, 8 Pl. Aurélie Nemours, 75013 Paris
  • LIX: École Polytechnique, Palaiseau, France, 1 Rue Honoré d'Estienne d'Orves, 91120 Palaiseau
  • LRE: EPITA, Paris, France, 14-16 Rue Voltaire, 94270 Le Kremlin-Bicêtre

Transportation

By Public Transport: All venues are accessible by public transportation.

  • IRIF: Metro line 14 to "Bibliothèque François-Mitterrand" or RER C to "Bibliothèque François-Mitterrand."
  • LIX: RER B train to "Massy-Palaiseau," then bus 91.06 or 91.10 to "Polytechnique."
  • LRE: Metro line 7 to "Kremlin-Bicêtre" or "Porte d'Italie"
